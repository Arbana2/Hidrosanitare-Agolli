1- STAKEHOLDERS (Përfituesit e sistemit)
Stakeholders

The system for Hidrosanitare Agolli will be used by several stakeholders who benefit from its implementation.

Internal Stakeholders

Administrator – Manages the entire system, controls user access and system settings.

Warehouse Manager – Monitors stock levels, product availability and supplier deliveries.

Sales Staff – Records daily sales and creates invoices for customers.

Finance/Accountant – Tracks payments, expenses, profit and generates financial reports.

Company Owner / Management – Uses reports to make business decisions and evaluate performance.

External Stakeholders

Customers – Purchase products and receive invoices and order confirmations.

Suppliers – Provide products and receive purchase orders from the company.
